In 1970, the World's best Weightlifters came to Columbus, Ohio to make an assault on Gravity. When they left, the 500 pound barrier stood no longer. But the most memorable lifter, by all accounts of those that were there, will always be that of a young Soviet Lifter, David Rigert. What follows are original photos by James E. Curry, Strength and Health's official photographer.
